ComfyLink turns your phone into a remote control for the ComfyUI running on your own computer or server. To use it you need: (1) a ComfyUI install you control, (2) the free ComfyUI-ComfyLink plugin, and (3) the ComfyLink mobile app.

Getting started

1. Install the ComfyUI-ComfyLink plugin

Install the plugin into your ComfyUI (via ComfyUI-Manager or by cloning into custom_nodes), then restart ComfyUI. Plugin repository and install instructions:

➡️ github.com/huijiutian/ComfyUI-ComfyLink

2. Pair your phone with your ComfyUI

Open the ComfyLink app, sign in, and follow the pairing flow. The plugin shows a pairing code / link; enter or scan it in the app to connect your phone to your ComfyUI through the ComfyLink relay. Pairing is a one-time step per device.

3. Run your workflows

Once paired, sync your workflow catalog, pick a workflow, and trigger it from your phone. Long or batch jobs keep running on your computer even if you close the app — reopen ComfyLink and progress resumes. When a job finishes, its outputs are downloaded to your device automatically.

FAQ

The app can't find or connect to my ComfyUI

  • Make sure ComfyUI is running and the ComfyUI-ComfyLink plugin is installed and loaded (check the ComfyUI console for the plugin's startup message).
  • Re-run the pairing flow; confirm you're signed into the same account on the app.
  • Check your computer has an internet connection.

An output failed with "too large"

Each tier has a per-generation size limit (Free 5 MB, Plus 50 MB, Pro 500 MB). Enable WebP optimization in settings to reduce size, or upgrade your tier.

Subscriptions, billing, restore, or cancellation

Subscriptions are billed by the Apple App Store or Google Play. Manage, restore, or cancel from your store account settings. See our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

快速上手

1. 安装 ComfyUI-ComfyLink 插件

把插件装进你的 ComfyUI(通过 ComfyUI-Manager,或 clone 到 custom_nodes),然后重启 ComfyUI。插件仓库与安装说明:

➡️ github.com/huijiutian/ComfyUI-ComfyLink

2. 把手机与 ComfyUI 配对

打开 ComfyLink App 登录,按配对流程操作。插件会显示配对码 / 链接;在 App 里输入或扫描即可通过 ComfyLink 中继把手机连到你的 ComfyUI。每台设备配对一次即可。

3. 跑你的工作流

配对后,同步工作流目录、选一个工作流,从手机触发。长时 / 批量任务即使你关掉 App 也会在电脑上继续跑 —— 重开 ComfyLink 自动接上进度。任务完成后,产物会自动下载到你的设备。

常见问题

App 找不到 / 连不上我的 ComfyUI

  • 确认 ComfyUI 正在运行,且 ComfyUI-ComfyLink 插件已安装并加载(看 ComfyUI 控制台是否有插件启动信息)。
  • 重新走一遍配对流程;确认 App 登录的是同一个账号。
  • 确认电脑有网络连接。

某次产物报「过大」失败

每档有单次出图体积上限(Free 5 MB、Plus 50 MB、Pro 500 MB)。在设置里开启 WebP 优化可减小体积,或升级档位。
