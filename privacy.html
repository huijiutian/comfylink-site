ComfyLink — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-24

ComfyLink ("the App", "we", "us") is a mobile companion for the user's own ComfyUI installation. We do not provide compute or GPU and we do not host an image-generation service — generation happens on hardware you own and control. This policy explains what data we handle and why.

1. Information we handle

  • Account data. Sign-in is handled by Supabase Auth. We store your email address and a user ID. You can sign in with Sign in with Apple or Google; if you use Apple's "Hide My Email" feature, we receive only the relayed/private address Apple provides.
  • Subscription status. Your subscription tier (Free / Plus / Pro) is stored in our database. Payments are processed by the Apple App Store or Google Play and managed through RevenueCat. We never receive or store your full payment-card details.
  • Device pairing. To connect the App to your own ComfyUI, our relay forwards pairing and job messages between your phone and your ComfyUI. We do not store your workflows on our servers beyond what is needed to relay them.
  • Generated outputs (images). To deliver results to your phone, outputs are stored temporarily on Cloudflare R2 and are automatically deleted after approximately 2 days.
  • Diagnostics. Basic technical logs may be processed to operate and secure the service.

2. Third-party processors

We rely on the following service providers, who process data on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication and database.
  • Cloudflare R2 — temporary storage of generated outputs (~2 days).
  • Render — hosting of our relay server.
  • RevenueCat — subscription management.
  • Apple / Google — payments and sign-in.

3. How we use information

We use the data above only to operate ComfyLink: to authenticate you, connect you to your own ComfyUI, deliver your outputs, manage your subscription, and keep the service secure. We do not sell or "share" (as those terms are defined under California law) your personal information, we do not use it for targeted or cross-context behavioral advertising, and we do not use third-party advertising SDKs.

4. International data transfers & security

ComfyLink is operated from Shanghai, China, while the infrastructure that stores and processes your data is provided by the third-party processors listed above, whose servers are located outside of China (primarily in the United States and other regions). As a result, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in countries other than the one in which you live — including the United States and, for operational and support purposes by our team, China. By using the App you consent to these transfers. We rely on our processors' contractual and technical safeguards to protect your data wherever it is processed.

Data in transit is encrypted with TLS. We apply reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information, though no system is perfectly secure.

5. Data retention

  • Generated outputs: ~2 days, then automatically deleted.
  • Account data: retained until you delete your account or request deletion.
  • Backups: retained for a limited period and then rotated out.

6. Your rights

You may request access to, or deletion of, your data by emailing us at support@huijiutian.com. You can also delete your account from within the App.

California residents (CCPA/CPRA). If you are a California resident, you have the right to: (i) know the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected about you in the prior 12 months, along with the sources and purposes; (ii) access and obtain a copy of that information; (iii) request deletion of your personal information; (iv) correct inaccurate personal information; and (v) not be discriminated against for exercising these rights. We have not sold or "shared" the personal information of California residents in the preceding 12 months, and we do not sell or share the personal information of minors under 16. To exercise your rights, email support@huijiutian.com with "California Privacy Rights" in the subject line; we will verify your identity before responding.

7. Children

ComfyLink is not directed to children. The App is rated 17+ and intended for adults.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email support@huijiutian.com.

ComfyLink — 隐私政策

最后更新:2026-06-24

ComfyLink(下称「本 App」「我方」)是用户「自己的」ComfyUI 的移动伴侣。我方不提供算力或 GPU,也不托管任何出图服务 —— 生成发生在你自有、自控的硬件上。本政策说明我方处理哪些数据以及原因。

1. 我方处理的信息

  • 账号数据。登录由 Supabase Auth 处理,我方存储你的邮箱地址与一个用户 ID。你可用「通过 Apple 登录」或 Google 登录;若你使用 Apple 的「隐藏邮箱」,我方仅收到 Apple 提供的中转 / 私有地址。
  • 订阅状态。你的订阅档位(Free / Plus / Pro)存于我方数据库。支付由 Apple App Store 或 Google Play 处理,并通过 RevenueCat 管理。我方从不接收或存储你的完整支付卡信息。
  • 设备配对。为把 App 连到「你自己的」ComfyUI,我方中继在你的手机与 ComfyUI 之间转发配对与任务消息。除转发所需外,我方不在服务器上存储你的工作流。
  • 生成产物(图片)。为把产物投递到你的手机,产物会临时存储在 Cloudflare R2 上,并在约 2 天后自动删除。
  • 诊断信息。为运营与保护服务,可能处理基础技术日志。

2. 第三方处理者

  • Supabase —— 认证与数据库。
  • Cloudflare R2 —— 生成产物的临时存储(约 2 天)。
  • Render —— 中继服务器托管。
  • RevenueCat —— 订阅管理。
  • Apple / Google —— 支付与登录。

3. 我方如何使用信息

我方仅为运营 ComfyLink 使用上述数据:为你认证、把你连到你自己的 ComfyUI、投递产物、管理订阅、保障服务安全。我方不会「出售」或「分享」(按加州法律定义)你的个人信息,不将其用于定向 / 跨背景行为广告,也不使用任何第三方广告 SDK。

4. 跨境数据传输与安全

ComfyLink 由位于中国上海的团队运营,而存储与处理你数据的基础设施由上述第三方处理者提供,其服务器位于中国境外(主要在美国及其它地区)。因此,你的信息可能被传输至你所在国以外的国家并在当地存储与处理 —— 包括美国,以及为运营与客服目的由我方团队在中国处理。使用本 App 即表示你同意此类传输。我方依赖处理者的合同与技术保障措施,在数据被处理的任何地点保护你的数据。

传输中的数据以 TLS 加密。我方采取合理的技术与管理措施保护你的信息,但没有系统是绝对安全的。

5. 数据保留

  • 生成产物:约 2 天,之后自动删除。
  • 账号数据:保留至你删除账号或请求删除。
  • 备份:保留有限期后轮换清除。

6. 你的权利

你可发邮件至 support@huijiutian.com 请求访问或删除你的数据,也可在 App 内删除账号。

加州居民(CCPA/CPRA)。若你是加州居民,你有权:(i) 知悉我方在过去 12 个月收集的个人信息类别与具体内容、来源及用途;(ii) 访问并获取该信息的副本;(iii) 请求删除你的个人信息;(iv) 更正不准确的个人信息;(v) 不因行使上述权利而受歧视性待遇。在过去 12 个月内,我方未出售或「分享」加州居民的个人信息,也不会出售或分享 16 岁以下未成年人的个人信息。行使权利请发邮件至 support@huijiutian.com,主题注明「California Privacy Rights」;我方会在响应前验证你的身份。

7. 儿童

ComfyLink 不面向儿童。本 App 分级 17+,面向成年人。

8. 政策变更
