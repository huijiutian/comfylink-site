ComfyLink

Your ComfyUI, anywhere.

ComfyLink is a mobile companion app for your own ComfyUI. Image generation runs entirely on hardware that you own and control — ComfyLink simply lets you drive it from your phone.

What you can do

  • Remotely trigger your ComfyUI workflows from your phone
  • Monitor generation progress in real time, and keep jobs running in the background
  • Have results delivered to your phone and saved to your own photo library
  • Organize reusable prompt presets and sync your workflow catalog

ComfyLink does not run any image generation on our servers and does not store your work long-term: generated results pass through a temporary staging area only to reach your device, then are automatically deleted.
